A Little About Us...

Wheelie clean UK has been established for over 25 years. We are a family run business working mainly around Manchester and surrounding areas. Our main business was always bin cleaning but due to changes over the years with the introduction of longer collection intervals and the adding of recycling bins we decided to diversify –  We now also clean UPVC and pressure wash most exteriors. Most of our work comes from repeat customers and recommendations. We take great pride in the jobs we do and are very dependable and conscientious.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do You Clean Bins In My Area?
Our bin cleaning services are only available in the following areas: Oldham (OL1, OL2, OL4, OL9) and Failsworth (M35). Other services are available further afield.
What Time Do You Come?
We can’t give a time every day is different but we try to be there as soon as possible.  (Bins need to be left out after emptying until our staff have been)
How Often Do You Clean My Bin?
Your bins are cleaned on a 3 weekly cycle.
What Day Do You Clean My Bin?
All chosen bins are cleaned on the day they are emptied
Which Bins Do You Clean?

We clean the bins you choose to be cleaned (see price guide).

When Do You Clean The Garden Bins?

We aim to clean the garden bin on the same day and at the same time as the brown bin.
